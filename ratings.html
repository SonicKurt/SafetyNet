Rating Board

  • The rating system for movies and video games helps parents providing with information about the content of movies or video games. It determines what movies or video games are appropriate.

  • Flim Ratings

    Each film has a rating that will indicate the level of content for parents deciding to let their children see this movie. All ratings have a brief description of the content in this movie. Film Ratings consists of G (General Audiences), PG (Parental Guidance Suggested), PG-13 (Parents Strongly Cautioned), R (Restricted), and NC-17 (Adults Only)

  • Game Ratings

    Like film ratings, video games have their own rating board. Video Games consist of Everyone, Everyone 10+, Teen, Mature, and Adults Only. On the back of every video game, it will give you a little description of the content in this video game
