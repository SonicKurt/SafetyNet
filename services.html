Services

  • Many web and application services have features for allowing parents to block access to any inappropriate content from their children.

  • YouTube

    Turn Restricted Mode On or Off

    1. First find your profile picture and click on your profile
    2. Next click Restricted Mode
    3. A dialog box should appear, you can use it to turn Restricted mode on or off

  • YouTube Kids

    If you have a child under 13 years old, there’s an app called YouTube Kids that only has content meant for young children. It automatically filters content that is not suitable for children and gives you access to parental controls.

  • Twitch

    If you decide Twitch is safe for your children, be on the platform with them to monitor the view and behavior.

    • Twitch allows you to configure the settings on your account so you can moderate who they talk to and block private messages from strangers
    • Remind your children to NEVER share any personal information online.

  • Duolingo

    Duolingo is a free app for ages 11+ who want to learn new languages. It is very educational and interactive.

  • Minecraft: Pocket Edition

    Minecraft: Pocket Edition: Minecraft is a video game designed for ages 10+ to create. Let’s you create just about anything you can build. The cost is $7 on the App Store.

  • Fisher-Price apps

    These consist of multiple free apps for iOS and Android devices made by Fisher-Price for children 2+. Activities range from learning to count, read, and rhyme.
