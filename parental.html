Parental Controls

  • Parental controls are features that allow parents to restrict the access of content to their children. There are two types of parental controls: Content Filters and Usage Controls.

  • Content Filters

    Parents can have filters that limit the content that their children are seeing on the internet. They can block content that could be inappropriate for the children to see depending on their age. If your children on social media sites, you should block any outgoing content. It will prevent sharing any information about your children online.

  • Usage Controls

    Parents can also control the amount of screen time, what apps are installed, and features enabled on your child’s device.

    Many operating systems can track the activity on your device. For instance, Apple has implemented ways on how much time you have been on your device and certain apps as well. Parents can block out certain apps from the App Store or the Goggle Play to be installed onto your child’s device. Video game companies have started to implement settings for parents to turn off the console at a certain time limit or block out any inappropriate video games to play on your video game systems.

  • Windows 10

    1. First, you need to set up a child account. Go to the settings menu.
    2. Click “Accounts.”
    3. Click “Family & other people tab” and click “Add a family member.”
    4. Click “Add a Child” and enter the information as needed.
    5. Next, go to your Microsoft account page. Click “Web browsing” and check the box “Only see websites on the allowed list.”
    6. Lastly, enter URLs for the sites you would like to block and click the “Block” button.

  • macOS

    1. Open “System Preferences” and click on “Parental Controls.”
    2. Create a new profile with parental controls or add the controls to the current user. If you want to create a new profile, select “Create a new user account with parental controls.”
    3. If the current profile is password-protected, enter your password. Once the new profile is created, go into Parental Controls and click the “Web” tab.
    4. If you want to specify want websites to be allowed or not allowed, Click on “Customize.” There will be two sections to add websites that will always be allowed or never allowed to access these websites.
    5. Click the addition sign under “Never allow” and enter the URL of the website you would like to block. Make sure to click “OK” when you’re finished.
